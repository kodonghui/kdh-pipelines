CEO provided 9 benchmark sites in UI-Benchmark/ folder:
- Linear (dark, minimal, modern tech)
- Figma (clean professional)
- 심팩그룹 (Korean corporate, sophisticated)
- 제네시스 Genesis (luxury, premium, elegant)
- 진인프라 (Korean IT, clean corporate)
- Design product site (modern showcase)
- WWIT Design / Lifeplus (Korean mobile UI/UX patterns)

**Why:** CEO rejected multiple Subframe-generated login designs as "별로" (not great), said my design taste is bad. These benchmarks define what "good" looks like.

**How to apply:** When making UI, reference Linear's dark minimalism + Genesis's premium feel + Korean corporate polish. Avoid generic SaaS colors. Prioritize: premium feel, clean typography, subtle animations, dark mode capability, Korean text harmony.
